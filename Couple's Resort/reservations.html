Power Outage Policy

Our Power Outage Policy was implemented following a significant outage in winter 2009, marking the first shutdown in 42 years due to power failure. During a power outage, we strive to ensure your comfort and convenience by offering the following services:

1. Wood burning fireplace in all accommodations, adding a touch of romance to your stay.

2. Fully operational kitchen for gourmet dining experiences.

3. Cold running water in all accommodations.

4. Hot and cold showers available in the gym/sauna facilities.

5. Recreation room with a large TV for essential entertainment.

6. Office access to the outside world with phone and data line services.

7. Please note that certain amenities such as toys in your accommodation and the pool/hot tub will not be operational during a power outage.

Additionally, we aim to keep you informed about the status of our services by updating our website's homepage promptly. In the event of a power outage during your stay, we will make every effort to notify you. If successful, we offer the option of refunding your deposit upon request.

Furthermore, should you experience a power outage while staying with us, we provide the following discounts based on the duration of the outage:

2-hour outage between 4pm and 11pm: 10% off

3-hour outage between 8pm and 11pm: 20% off

4-hour outage between 7pm and 11pm: 30% off

5-hour outage between 6pm and 11pm: 35% off plus one free night stay in the future, with similar accommodation

Please note that power outages occurring after 11pm until 4pm the following day will not be eligible for discounts.
