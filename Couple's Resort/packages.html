Eco Friendly Resort

Eco Friendly Resort

The Algonquin Spa operates entirely on organic and solar power principles, ensuring an eco-friendly experience for our guests:


We provide only 100% organic towels and linens sourced from Dream Designs. Our facials, wraps, and body molding products are exclusively from Eminence Brand and are 100% organic.

Solar panels onsite power 100% of the Algonquin Spa's energy needs. We use only wool carpets or bamboo flooring, both renewable resources. Our staff, including room service, maintenance, cleaners, gardeners, and sport center employees, utilize a fleet of 8 electric golf carts. Firewood used in 60 fireplaces throughout Couples Resort and Adventure Lodge is carbon-neutral, unlike natural gas or propane.

At the “Bear Trail” Dining Room at Couples Resort, Algonquin Park: We produce vegetable food products onsite. All organic kitchen waste is composted and used in our gardens. No pesticides are used in our vegetable or flower gardens.
