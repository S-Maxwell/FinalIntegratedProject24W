Summary About Couple's Resort

At Lakeside Patio & Pool, enjoy serene dining with picturesque views of Galeairy Lake. Our service patio offers meals, snacks, and drinks served by attentive staff in a licensed, non-smoking environment, with comfortable wrought iron furniture. Alternatively, visit our self-service patios overlooking the lake, where you can bring your refreshments. Choose between our double fire-pit patio for smoking or the glass-free, non-smoking pool patio. Relax in our outdoor salt-water pool, open from May to Labour Day Weekend, with temperatures set at 82°F & 28°C. Explore our Rec Room Games Area and gift shop, offering snacks, toiletries, books, and souvenirs. With recreational facilities like a gym, sports center, and museum, our resort promises an unforgettable stay filled with history and cherished memories.

Art Gallery at Couple's Resort

Gertrud Sorensen is an artist who was born in Austria and now lives in Canada. She used to run a business with her husband, Fritz, at The Bear Trail Couples Resort, but now she focuses on her art. Gertrud has a big studio where she has over 30 paintings displayed. Her paintings show the different seasons in Algonquin Park with lots of colors. She learned from other artists and got inspiration from nature. You can see her paintings in her studio at The Bear Trail Couples Resort. If you visit, you can even watch her paint and maybe buy one of her paintings as a special gift.

Our Facilities

About our Facilities

Lakeside Patio & Pool: One patio area overlooking Galeairy Lake where you can enjoy meals, snacks, and drinks served by our staff. The area is licensed for alcohol by the LLBO and is non-smoking. We have wrought iron tables and chairs for your comfort.

Self-Service Patios: Three patio areas also overlooking Galeairy Lake where you can bring your own refreshments. 1. Double fire-pit patio where smoking is allowed. 2. Pool patio where glass is not allowed and is non-smoking. 3. Couples chaise lounges patio which is also non-smoking.

Pool: Outdoor salt-water pool measuring 20 feet by 40 feet. Open from May Long Weekend until Labour Day Weekend, with a temperature of 82°F & 28°C, operating hours from 10 am to 7 pm.

Rec Room Games Area: Gift shop & rec room where you can purchase gift certificates online and find snacks, toiletries, books, Eminence Spa products, souvenirs, and more.
Rec room includes 3 slate billiard tables, 1 ping pong table, 1 foosball table, 1 shuffleboard table, 2 dart boards, and 2 seating areas.

Gym & Sports Center: Equipment includes a Concept 2 rowing machine, variable stationary bike, tread master, free weights, mats, TV/VCR, water cooler, sauna, change rooms, showers, and on-site washer and dryer.

Sports Center: We provide all the necessary equipment for outdoor activities throughout the seasons, including all-weather clothing, outerwear, footwear, hats, accessories, apparel, motor boats, paddle boats, row boats, kayaks, canoes, pickleball, tennis, badminton, and basketball courts and equipment. Most equipment is free; for details, check out our Packages Page.

Museum: Take a historical photo journey from one of the first cabins built on the property when it was known as the Bear Trail Inn to the present day Couples Resort. The resort has a rich history since 1967 when the Sorensen family purchased the property.

Resort offers an unforgettable experience that creates lasting memories, keeping guests coming back for more.
